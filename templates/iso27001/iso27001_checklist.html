How to reach ISO 27001 compliance

For many organizations, obtaining ISO 27001 certification is a significant step toward expanding their business and entering new markets. To comply with ISO 27001, you must establish a strong and strategic information security management system (ISMS), which includes implementing data security best practices. We've created a comprehensive checklist to guide your ISO 27001 implementation and certification. This checklist includes the following steps:

  1. Create a road map for your ISMS implementation and ISO 27001 certification
  2. Determine the scope of your organization's ISMS
  3. Set up an ISMS team and assign roles
  4. Create an inventory of information assets
  5. Conduct a risk assessment
  6. Create a risk register
  7. Create a risk treatment plan
  8. Complete the Statement of Applicability Worksheet
  9. Implement ISMS policies and regularly assess risk
  10. Set up employee training and awareness programs
  11. Gather necessary documents and records
  12. Conduct an internal audit against ISO 27001 standards
  13. Conduct an external audit of your ISMS to obtain ISO 27001 certification
  14. Address any violations.
  15. Conduct regular management reviews
  16. Prepare for subsequent ISO 27001 and surveillance audits
  17. Consider automating the ISO 27001 certification process
