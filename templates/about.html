What is our project about?

Our capstone project is designed to empower individuals and organizations by providing essential resources related to cybersecurity certifications, including SOC and NIST. We aim to simplify the complex landscape of compliance by offering a comprehensive checklist that outlines the necessary steps for achieving certification. Our goal is to make the certification process more accessible and manageable, ensuring that users have the tools and knowledge they need to meet industry standards and enhance their cybersecurity posture.

What does our website provide?

Our website serves as a comprehensive resource for navigating the world of cybersecurity compliance. It features concise summaries of key compliance frameworks such as SOC 1, SOC 2, and NIST, providing you with a clear understanding of their requirements and significance. In addition, we offer a practical checklist designed to guide you through each step of the compliance process. Whether you're seeking to grasp the essentials of popular certifications or need a structured approach to achieving compliance, our website is your go-to destination for streamlined, accessible information.
